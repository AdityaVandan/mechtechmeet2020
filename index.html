Mech Tech Meet

About

MTM

Mech Tech Meet is the initiative of students of Ujjain Engineering College,ujjain to encourage creativity and innovation among engineering students.started in the year 2010,Mech Tech Meet has become an epitome of student level technical competetion in Central India.

The events are designed so as to challenge students with complex situations and make them sport their true calibre. In the due course of journey,Mech Tech Meet in it's 10th spell is ready with even more challenging and thrilling competitions in it's basket.We at Mech Tech Meet try to build and develop a 'techno-logical' spirit in the students to encourage them towards the ideal 'Technology for society'.

Our Theme

Think Green

Go Green

Mech Tech Meet has been a great platform for the technophiles to show their engineering skills and creativity. Since the begining of MTM, we are focused towards promoting the issues related to sustainable use of the available resources. Now the environmental problems are growing upto a level of existential crisis.We all have to take our responsibilities and move towards sustainablity and use of other green energy resources.
